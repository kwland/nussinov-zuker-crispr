Part 1: Topic review

Why RNA folding matters

Biology

RNA is single-stranded, so it can bend back and pair with itself. Stems, loops, and bulges affect how an RNA molecule behaves, including whether a CRISPR guide can bind its DNA target cleanly.

Computation

Nussinov folding uses dynamic programming. It solves small subsequences first, stores their best scores, and combines them to find one high-scoring non-crossing structure.

CRISPR

For SpCas9, the guide spacer is usually 20 nt. The PAM-proximal seed is especially important, so the project tracks whether the last 8 bases are predicted to be paired or accessible.

Three build steps

  1. Implement Nussinov folding and traceback in Python and JavaScript.
  2. Convert folds into CRISPR features such as seed accessibility and GC content.
  3. Compare known guides, random controls, and bad controls with the same pipeline.
